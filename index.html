Swapnil DevOps Project

Project Overview

  • Amazon S3 is used to store our static website content, ensuring durability and scalability.
  • CloudFront acts as our CDN to enhance website performance and reduce latency.
  • ACM handles SSL/TLS certificates to secure the website.
  • CodePipeline automates our CICD pipeline for seamless deployment.
  • AWS Route53 ensures reliable DNS management for our domain.
